Mr. Speaker, I do not despair of my coun-

I appeal to hon. members to vote for the

forging out the great destiny for which we
have been created. In the name of peace

to force this measure through the House in
its present form, but to amend it as pro-
posed by the leader of the opposition.

Again I appeal to every member of this
