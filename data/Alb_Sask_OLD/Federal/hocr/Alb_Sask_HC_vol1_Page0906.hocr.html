Everything of that kind is to be wiped out ;

determine what kind of constitution, in the

guide the destinies of that country for all
time to come. I think this proposition has
been made clear by very high legal authori-
ty. I do not like to repeat over and over
again the opinions of public men, but I
think we make no mistake if we repeat
daily the opinions of some of our public

the minds of hon. members of this House,
some of whom do not yet apprehend them.
Whether they'have not listened to them or
