NORTH-WEST TERRITORIES ACT.

Mr. McCARTHY moved for leave to in- troduce Bill (No. 15) to amend the North- west Territories Act. He said : The pur- pose of this Bill is to confer upon the Legis- lative Assembly of the North-west Territo- ries full and complete power in the matter of education.

Motion agreed to, and Bill read the first time.
