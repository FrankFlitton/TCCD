The MINISTER OF JUSTICE. The effectwould be to confer on the North-west assem-bly power to regulate all matters connectedwith the civil jurisdiction of the province.That is to say, they may determine the sit-tings of the court, the places where thecourt is to sit, how often it is to sit : theymay provide for the sittings of the court inbanc or with a single judge; provide for thedivision of the territory into judicial dis-tricts ; define the territorial jurisdiction ofthe judges, and so on—all matters relatingto the civil organization of the court. Ithink it is a step in the right direction.

Amendment agreed to.
