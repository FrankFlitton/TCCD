In 1894 cer-tain gentlemen from the North-west whotook an interest in politics, and werestrongly impressed that a wrong had beendone by those unscrupulous persons swear-ing themselves on the list, urged that achange should be made in the directionwhich the reform of the law took in 1894 ;and an amendment to that effect was placedin my hands when the Bill of 1894 was pass-ing through the House.
