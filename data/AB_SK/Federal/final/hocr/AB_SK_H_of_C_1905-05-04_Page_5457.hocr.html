Mr. TURRIFF. We do not get the max- imum when we attain a population of 800,- 000, but when we attain a population of 1,200,000 in each province. When we get the maximum, or a little over $2,000,000, if the people of the Territories will then be paying as much revenue to the Dominion per capita as they are now, the Dominion government will be receiving from each of these provinces a sum of about $10,000,000 or $12,000,000, so that I do not think the debt of the Dominion will be increased by the subsidy.

Mr. SPROULE. What is the cost of the government there today ? I remember pointing out last year what we spent.
