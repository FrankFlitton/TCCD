The government, therefore, are justified in establishing the provinces of Saskatche- wan and Alberta. In so doing, they are only following in the wake of the mar- vellous progress which has been the result of their own efforts. I am proud, Mr. Speaker, to have a seat in this parliament which, by carving out these new provinces, is turning a new leaf in the history of Canada. These two new sister provinces, entering confederation, will strengthen the bounds which unite old and new Canada, and bind more firmly together, through mutual interest, all the provinces of Canada.
