1889. COMMONS DEBATES. 105

THE NORTH-WEST TERRITORIES ACT.

Mr. DAVIN asked, Whether it is the intention of the Government to bring in a Bill amending the North-West Territories Act, so as to secure to the Territories Govern- ment powers as full as those of the Provinces, with the exception of the power of borrowing money ?

Sir JOHN A. MACDONALD. It is not the intention of the Government to bring in such a Bill this Session.
