601 [SEPTEMBER 2, 1896]

ORGANIZATION OF THE NORTH-WEST
TERRITORIES.

On the order being called:

That the time has arrived when the North-west
Territories should be treated on the same footing
as the organized provinces, that an annual sub-
sidy should be voted for carrying on the govern-
ment of the Territories, which subsidy should
be calculated on the same grounds as those on
which the subsidy voted to the province of Mani-
toba is based.

Mr. DAVIN. The ground of this motion
has already been occupied by a motion of
my hon. friend from Alberta. If my hon.
friend had informed me that he had given
notice of the motion. I would not have
given notice of this. I shall not move this
motion. ;
