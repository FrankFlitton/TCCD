Incidentally, Mr. Speaker, I am not entering
into a controversy on the question whether
confederation was a pact, a treaty, a contract
or a statute; but both Sir Wilfrid Laurier and
the late Right Hon. Ernest Lapointe used the

pointe continues as follows:

As I have said. I do not think there could be any

I do not think there can be any attempt to change
