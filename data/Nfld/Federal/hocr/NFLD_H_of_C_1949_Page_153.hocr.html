Mr. Knowles: Hear, hear.

Mr. St. Laurent: But this is a pension
which is payable to those who require it,
according to the standards set by the legisla-
tion. The pensions are administered by the
provincial governments and it is they who
determine whether or not an individual
comes within the category determined by the
means test. The responsibility of the federal
government under the federal legislation is
to reimburse to the province three-quarters
of the amount it has in fact paid out, up to a
maximum of $30 a month for each pensioner.
