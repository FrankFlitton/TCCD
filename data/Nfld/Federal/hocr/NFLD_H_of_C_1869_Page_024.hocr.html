bore various rates of interest—4, 5, 5%, and 6
per cent.

assets to represent the debt. What property
was to be handed over?

handed over. The average receipts from the
Customs for the ﬁve years previous to 1867,
were from $510,000 to $530,000; and in 1868
$634,000.
