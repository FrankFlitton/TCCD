EMPLOYMENT—DISPLACED PERSONS

Mr. Stewart (Winnipeg North):

1. How many displaced persons have completed
their work contracts in Canada?

2. Is there any special employment policy for
these people?

Mr. Cote (Verdun):

1. 6,629.

2. On completion of undertaking, displaced
persons may accept employment of their own
choosing in the same manner as other resi-
dents. They are encouraged to remain in the
employment to which they were assigned but
are informed that the facilities of the national
employment service are available to them it
they wish to take other work. Local ofﬁces
of the national employment service have been
instructed to interview such applicants care-
fully to ensure that the most suitable possible
placements are made having regard to the
applicants’ wishes and skills or trades
possessed.
