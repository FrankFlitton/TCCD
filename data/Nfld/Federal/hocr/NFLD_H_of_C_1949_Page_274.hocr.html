FARM LOAN BOARD—BRITISH COLUMBIA

Mr. Pearkes:
1. At what points in Canada are branches of the
farm loan board located?

2. Who is responsible for the operation of the
board on Vancouver island, British Columbia?

3. How many applications for loans were made
from British Columbia last year?

4. How many of these were approved?

Mr. _Belzile:

1. New Westminster, British Columbia;
Edmonton, Alberta; Regina, Saskatchewan;
Winnipeg, Manitoba; Toronto, Ontario; Que-
bec, Quebec; Saint John, New Brunswick;
Halifax, Nova Scotia; Charlottetown, Prince
Edward Island.

2. Mr. W. S. Palmer, Branch Manager,
Canadian farm loan board, New Westminster,
B.C.

3. 229.

4. Ninety-one have been approved for loan
and 28 were still pending as of February 11,
1949. Of the 28 pending applications, 17 of
the securities offered have not as yet been
appraised.
