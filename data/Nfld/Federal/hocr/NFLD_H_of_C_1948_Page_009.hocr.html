POSTAL SERVICE

MAIL CONTRACTS—SUPPLEMENTAL PAYMENTS ACT
AMENDMENT

Hon. ERNEST BERTRAND (Postmaster
General) moved that the house go into com-
mittee at the next sitting to consider the
following resolution:

That it is expedient to bring in a measure to
amend the Supplemental Payments Act to pro-
vide that no supplemental payments shall be
authorized under the act after the thirty~ﬁrst
day of March, 1949.

He said: His Excellency the Governor
General, having been made acquainted with
the subject matter of this resolution, recom-
mends it to the consideration of the house.

Motion agreed to.
