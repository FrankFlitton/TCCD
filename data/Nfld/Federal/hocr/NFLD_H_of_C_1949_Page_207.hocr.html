The point I am trying to make is this:
Today we are not dealing so much with the
expedition of the entry of Newfoundland into
confederation as we are with the greater and
broader proposition whether our constitution
is not the thing within which we live,
whether our constitution is not the thing that

the words of the hon. member for Rosetown-
