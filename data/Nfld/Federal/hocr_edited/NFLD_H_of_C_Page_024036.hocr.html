did not think, however, that the revenue
would fall from $600,000 to $300,000, as
alleged by the member for Westmorland.
That could not well be when the rates of

rum, gin, whiskey, brandy, sugar, molasses,
tobacco, etc. On tea, also, there was a small
difference on certain kinds. From the most
careful estimates he had been able to obtain,
