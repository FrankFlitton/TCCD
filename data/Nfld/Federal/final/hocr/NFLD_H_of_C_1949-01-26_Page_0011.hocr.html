Some hon. Members: Oh, oh.

Mr. St. Laurent: Hon. members can say “Oh, oh” all they like, but that is the fact. I know the intent of our desires just as well as hon. members who are saying “Oh, oh”. I wish to meet in the greatest degree possible the convenience of all hon. members in the house. I do not want to start a debate today on the reasons for proceeding immediately with the Newfoundland measure; that will come on Friday. If we cannot arrange some- thing that appears to be satisfactory to the majority of the members of the house, we will arrange something else. There is no inten- tion of getting a snap decision of the house, but I do not think it would be appropriate for me to ask the house to make one order today and then come back on Friday and ask it to vary that order.
