HOUSE OF COMMONS

 

Thursday, March 18, 1948

The house met at three o’clock.

POSTAL SERVICE

MAIL CONTRACTS—SUPPLEMENTAL PAYMENTS ACT AMENDMENT

Hon. ERNEST BERTRAND (Postmaster General) moved that the house go into com- mittee at the next sitting to consider the following resolution:

That it is expedient to bring in a measure to amend the Supplemental Payments Act to pro- vide that no supplemental payments shall be authorized under the act after the thirty~ﬁrst day of March, 1949.

He said: His Excellency the Governor General, having been made acquainted with the subject matter of this resolution, recom- mends it to the consideration of the house.

Motion agreed to.

HONG KONG

REFERENCE OF DOCUMENTS T0 JOINT COMMITTEE ON PRINTING

Right Hon. W. L. MACKENZIE KING (Prime Minister) moved:

That the documents tabled on March 10, 1948, relating to the inquiry made pursuant to order in council P.C. 1160, of February 12, 1942, into the dispatch of the Canadian expeditionary force to the crown colony of Hong Kong, be referred to the joint committee on printing for report under standing order 64.

Motion agreed to.
