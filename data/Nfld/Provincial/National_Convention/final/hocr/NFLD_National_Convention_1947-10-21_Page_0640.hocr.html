Mr. Chairman I ought to remind members that under rule 50, this Committee has the right to invite any person or persons before it to assist them in the preparation of the report under con- sideration. I think you can safely assume, that the

Committee has taken due cognisance of all the matters referred to by members this afternoon, and that they will be dealt with. The manner of course in which they will be dealt with will be known to us all when the report is introduced and tabled for discussion.
