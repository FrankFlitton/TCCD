Mr. Bailey $75 instead of $122. Well it’s only $3 out. Now then, we will take capacity to pay. I think if anybody here understands these two places I do. We will take Fortune and Shelbume. We will take the municipal taxes in the two places. I believe they have got a town council in Fortune and I believe the government is paying half of that town council for the current year, Then if we go over to Shelbume, a town with practically the same earning capacity as Fortune, we ﬁnd they have got a population of 1,605, they have 50 sidewalks, 3.5 miles of asphalt, and no stone or gravel roads. And we ﬁnd out they are spending $19,299 for municipal purposes, and $16,200 to educate their children. They have a total tax levied of 61%. The cost for each pupil is $61 per year.
