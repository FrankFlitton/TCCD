Informal Session of Convention
Tuesday, February 4th, 1947
at 4.50 pm

Pursuant to adjournment of the public session at
430 pm the Convention assembled in the Legis-
lative Council Chamber to consider the im-
plementing of the motion of Hon. RB. Job.

Mr. Fogwill asked that the committee referred
to in Hon. Mr. Job’s motion be elected in open
session.

Mr. Higgins, KC. moved and Mr. Newell
seconded that a nominating committee be elected
for the purpose of recommending and nominating
a committee to interview the Governor in Com-
mission.

Ater some discussion Mr. Higgins, K.C,, with

the consent of Mr. Newell and the Convention
withdrew his motion and moved that the electing
of that committee be effected forthwith by secret
ballot.

The motion was seconded by Rev. Mr. Burry
and carried, whereupon the following were
elected members of the committee with the
Chairman: Hon. RB. Job, Messrs. CA. Crosbie,
G.F. Higgins, K,C,, T.G.W. Ashbourne, J.R.
Smallwood, I. Newell and FD. Fogwill.

There being no further business the meeting
then adjourned.
