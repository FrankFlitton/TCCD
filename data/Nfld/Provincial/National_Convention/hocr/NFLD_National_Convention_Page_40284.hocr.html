January 1947

NATIONAL CONVENTION 267

Mr. Smallwood I was wondering the past few
weeks what the four members from Placentia Bay
were looking so happy about I think it may be
because these four gentlemen have succeeded in
getting a whole lot of things for Placentia Bay.
They are getting a new boat, and a new bait depot,
and a new cottage hospital, so that must be why
they are so happy. They have even got roads, a

couple of million dollars will be spent on them
Mrs Chairman, I move that the committee rise,
report progress and ask leave to sit again.

[The committee of the whole rose and reported
progress. The Convention received and adapted
the report of the Committee on Transportation
and Communications, and adjourned until the
call of the Chair]
