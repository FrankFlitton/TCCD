those who compile, at no doubt considerable
expense, and furnish to the broadcasting com—
panies the various news bulletins given to the
public free of charge. These bulletins are ap-
preciated in the city of St. John’s and especially
in those outports where no daily newspapers are
immediately available. I mention the Gerald
S. Doyle news bulletin1 and the bulletin supplied
by Messrs Harvey and Co.2 VOCM is not very
much heard in Twillingate, probably on account
of lower power. I see no reason why they should
not receive permission to increase their power.
The weather forecasts, news regarding steamer
