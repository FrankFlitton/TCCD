Mr. Chairman It is a very important question
of course, Major Cashin.

Mr. Smallwood I would like to add my voice to

occasions on which Major Cashin has raised the
matter of what would happen to the government
Savings Bank under confederation, and who
would control the amount of interest that might
be paid on deposits, I now, more then he, am
anxious that the question be answered. I know
what the answer will be, but I want that answer
brought in to settle the matter once and for all.
