The Selkirk Treaty.

 

CHAPTER I.

THE SELKIRK TREATY.

In the year 1811, the Earl of Selkirk purchased* from the Governor and Company of Adventurers trading into Hud- son’s Bay, in consideration of ten shillings and certain agree- ments and understandings contained in the Indenture, a large tract of territory within Rupert’s Land described in the Inden- ture as follows:
