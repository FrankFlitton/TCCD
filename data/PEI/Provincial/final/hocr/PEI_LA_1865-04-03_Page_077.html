SPEECH

Mr. President and Honorable Gentlemen of theLegislative Council :

Mr. Speaker and Gentlemen of the House of Assembly :

The unremitting attention which you have bestowedon your Legislative duties, enables me to relieve you,at an earlier period than usual, from further attenancehere.

After deliberate consideration of the Terms adoptedby the Conference at Quebec as a basis for Confedera-tion of the British North American Provinces, youhave refused, on the part of this Colony, to sanctionthe proposed Scheme.

In accordance with your request, I shall forward tothe Secretary of State for the Colonies your address toHer Majesty on that subject.Â
