1866 PARLIAMENTARY REPORTER 55   

Thursday, May 10.     

The Committee appointed to prepare an Address to Her Majesty the Queen, expressive of the views of the House   of Assembly upon the subject of Confederation, submitted an Address, which was adopted.    

An address was also prepared and adopted, requesting His Ex- cellency the   Lieut. Governor to be pleased to forward to Her Majesty the Queen the said Address of the House of    Assembly on Colonial Confederation  

House adjourned.

A. McNEILL. Reporter.
