1873 PARLIAMENTARY REPORTER. 53

The Hon. J. C. POPE, a member ofHer Majesty's Government, appearedat the Bar with a message from HisHonor the Lieutenant Governor.

On motion, the message was re-ceived and read by his honor theSpeaker. Said message contained theCorrespondence between the late gov-ernment and the Governor General ofCanada, in reference to the proposedunion of this Island with the Domin-ion, and was read at the Clerk's table.

On motion, it was laid on the table,and its consideration made the order ofthe day for Monday next.
