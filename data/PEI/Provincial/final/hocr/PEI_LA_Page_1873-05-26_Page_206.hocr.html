He(Mr. L.) did not boast of the superiorintelligence and wealth of the peopleof Queen's County, compared withthose of the outlying Counties, butcontended that man for man, they wereas intelligent and as wealthy as thepeople of either Prince County orKing's County, and, therefore, were,on that ground, entitled to representa-tion according to their number.
