This Island would neverhave objected to the payment of the salary of theLieut. Governor, if we had been placed in thesame position as the other Colonies were. ThisDespatch plainly proves that when the demandwas made, in 1865, when confederation was theorder of the day, that it was with a view to ourbeing placed in the Dominion.
