After recess,

THIRD READINGS

On motion of Mr. Langlois, the Bill to
amend the Act respecting Pilots for below
the harbour of Quebec was read a third time
and passed.

On motion of Hon. Mr. Holton, the Bill to
amend the Act incorporating the Canadian
and British Telegraph Company was read a
second time. Considered in Committee of the
Whole and read a third time and passed.
