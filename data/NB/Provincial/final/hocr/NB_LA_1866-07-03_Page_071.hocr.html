The difficultywe always find is, the people do not de-sire to be taxed even for the advantageof the country. Suppose five personal go into part-nership and they agree each to put in anequal amount, and they are each allowedto draw out a certain amount for perso-nal expenses every year, and every in-crease should be added to the commonfund for the general good. The faster the ge-neral fund increases the better for thewhole if it is increased without increa-sing taxation upon the people.
