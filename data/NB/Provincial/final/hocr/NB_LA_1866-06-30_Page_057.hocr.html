MONDAY,, July 2d

NORTH SHORE STEAMER.

Hon. Mr. McCLELAN in reply to aquestion put by the hon. member fromGloucester (Mr. Young) said that a bar-gain had been made with Canada for theplacing of a steamer on the North Shortroute, to call at various ports. Thecontract was not yet signed but there wasno doubt the steamer would be put on.
