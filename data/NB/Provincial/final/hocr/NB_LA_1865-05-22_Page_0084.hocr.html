should be no draft at all, because therewill be hundreds of volunteers from allparts of the Province. The same argu-ments that apply to the fishermen willapply with equal force to the farmer,manufacturer and lumberman. The farm-er may be prevented from putting in hiscrop. Mr. Wetmore. - This is a very reason-able proposition, and should be extendedto all fishermen instead of those only whoare engaged in the deep sea fisheries, forI do not see any reason why they shouldbe exempt more than the others.
