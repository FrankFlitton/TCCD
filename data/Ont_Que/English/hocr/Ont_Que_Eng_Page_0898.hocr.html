I invite those gentlemen to examine with a little more attention the map of the province as far as its lower ex- tremity—the Bay of Chaleurs and Gaspé, and they will perceive that it contains a tolerably vast territory and good land adapt- ed for colonization—a fact of which they may also convince themselves by glancing at the colonization reports.
