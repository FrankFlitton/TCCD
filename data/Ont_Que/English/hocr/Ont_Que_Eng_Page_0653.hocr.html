That is another reason why I wish to see no delay in the union and in the amalgamation of the British provinces, in order that we may at once consolidate our- selves into one people, and at. once endeavor to abolish those barriers which now exist between us, and develope the feeling that we have com- mon interests, and that we are dependent the one upon the other, which can never be the case so long as division walls exist. It seems really astonishing to my mind that any man who really desires to see built up on this con- tinent a nationality independent of the United States, should offer any opposition to the pro- posal now before us.
