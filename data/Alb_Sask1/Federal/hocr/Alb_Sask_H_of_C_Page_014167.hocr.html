CIRCULATION OF AMERICAN CURRENCY.

Mr. BICKERDIKE asked:

1. Is the government aware that a very large
amount of American currency is at present in
circulation at par in Canada ?

2. Is it within the knowledge of the govern-
ment that in most cases Canadian currency is
refused in the United States, and when accept-
ed, only taken at a very heavy discount ?

3. Is it the intention of the government to
introduce a measure the present session for
the purpose of preventing the circulation of
United States coin in the Dominion ?

Hon. W. S. FIELDING (Minister of Fin—
ance) :

1. Yes.

2. Yes.

3. It is not the intention of the govern-
ment to introduce any measure to prohibit
the circulation of American coin, but we -
anticipate that we will be able to make

such regulations as will largely displace

that coin.
