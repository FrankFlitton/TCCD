machinery of the British North America
Act would have applied to them. The Act
that was passed to meet this case is known
as the British North America Act of 1871.
By section 4 of that Act, it is expressly
provided :

The parliament of Canada may from time
to time make provision for the administration,
peace. order and good government of any ter-
ritory not for the time being included in any
province.

the British North America Act of 1871 was
given full power to give these Territories
such laws as, in its judgment, seemed wise.
during the time they were territories. Can-

1875. Powers of local self—government were
