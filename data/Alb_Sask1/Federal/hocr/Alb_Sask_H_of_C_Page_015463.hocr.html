and extending to the Arctic ocean.

.The Bill to amend the Keewatin Act will
add to the present district of Keewatin (1)

sent provisional district of Mackenzie which
is not to be included in the new district;
and g3) the territory lying to the east of the
district of Keewatin as now constituted and
extending to Hudson Bay.
