dent to any person at all acquainted with
such matters that there is always some risk
in issuing, on progress estimates, interim
bonds, as the amount may far exceed the
value of the work as ﬁnally ascertained.
As I say, I pointed this out in the Railway
Committee to the Minister of Justice and in
