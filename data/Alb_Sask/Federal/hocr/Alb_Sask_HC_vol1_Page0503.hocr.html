EXTENSION OF THE BOUNDARIES OF MANITOBA.

On the Orders of the Day being called,

Rt. Hon. Sir WILFRID LAURIER (Prime Minister). I beg to lay upon the table of the House a return supplementary to the return which was laid on the table of the House on Monday last with respect to the claims of the government of Manitoba for an extension of the boundaries of that pro- vince. At the same time I move that the rules of the House be suspended, and that the return be printed forthwith.
