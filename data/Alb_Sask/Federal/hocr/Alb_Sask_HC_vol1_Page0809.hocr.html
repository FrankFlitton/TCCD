In the ﬁrst place, Mr. Christie, as Speaker of the Senate, has no right to express an opin- ion except by means of his casting vote in case of an equal division; then Mr. Letellier, being one of the ministers, could not reject in the Senate a principle endorsed by the gov— ernment and set forth in a Government Bill.
