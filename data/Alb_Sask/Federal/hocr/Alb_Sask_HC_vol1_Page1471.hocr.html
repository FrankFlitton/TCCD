They are a high-minded people, many of them have gone in from the old province of Ontario, from the pro- vinces of Nova Scotia and New Brunswick, many of them have come from the United States and from the British Isles, from Ger- many and other European countries, and they will say, why is this thus ? Why are we treated as serfs ? Some of them have come from Russia and other European coun- tries, and they will say, we lived a long time in serfdom, we came to a free country, and we are not allowed to call our own our own, we are not allowed to call this country our own.
