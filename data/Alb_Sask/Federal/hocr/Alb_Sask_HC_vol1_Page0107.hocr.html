THE OTTAWA ELECTRIC COMPANY.

' House in Committee on Bill (No. 12) re-
specting the Ottawa Electric Company.
On the preamble,

Mr. ROBERT STEWART (Ottawa). Up
to the present moment we have heard
nothing from the promoter of the Bill as to
the reasons why he asks this House to pass
it. As one of the representatives of the city
of Ottawa I wouid like to hear from him
why he asks for this legislation, which, to my
mind, is an invasion of the rights of the
municipality. In such an important matter
as this I really think that the promoter of
the Bill ought to give us some reasons why
he thinks we should pass it. I may say that
since this Bill was in committee before a
very numerously signed petition has been
sent in protesting against the passing of the
Bill, a petition signed by .over 2,000 rate—
payers of the city of Ottawa. I mention this
as showing the very strong feeling that ex-
ists in the city against this Bill; and yet in
face of this fact. and in face of the fact that
this Bill seems to call for the abolition of the
competition which exists to-day, we are
still without any reasons given by the pro»
moter of the Bill why it should pass.
