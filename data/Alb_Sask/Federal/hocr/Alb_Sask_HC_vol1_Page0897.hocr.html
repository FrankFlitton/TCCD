The public meeting held last Monday night
on the proclamation of the mayor in response

Autonomy Bill now before the parliament ’ was
one of the most largely attended gatherings
yet held in the city hall. Mayor Laird presided,

The mayor who presided, is one of the
leading Conservatives of the city of Regina,
and the promoters of the meeting were large-

is performing a service to Canada which all
peace loving and progressive citizens will
in a very short time‘ learn to appreciate
and applaud.
