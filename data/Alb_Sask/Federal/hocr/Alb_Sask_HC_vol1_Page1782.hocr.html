There was in the ordinance of 1892, a clause relating to the organization of the districts which remained what it had been in the previous ordinance; that is that the Catholics when they were a local minority in any district were still under the dis position which was denounced by Sir John Thompson in 1890 under which the Catho- lics could organize a minority separate school only when the majority had organized a public school.
