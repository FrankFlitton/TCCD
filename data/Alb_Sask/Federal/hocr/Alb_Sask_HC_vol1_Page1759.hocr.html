The Act of 1875, I may observe, which is the organic law of the provinces up to this moment, was not a school Act, it was simply an organic law. It did not pretend to frame a school law for the Territories, it did not pretend to enact a school system, it simply put forth certain rights which were to be pilots to, the legislative power when it came to exercise its authority. It imposed certain restrictions on the legislative powers of the Territories which they were bound to observe. Now what were these restrictions? I cannot do better than give the very words‘ of the Act itself. The Act stated this :

The legislature of the said province shall pass. all necessary laws in respect of education, and that it shall therein always be provided:
