That the native tribes have diminished in
numbers during the past two centuries is
without doubt, though there is some difﬁculty

as to the exact causes. On this point no par—

pany have not hesitated to take whatever

the Indians. Under the Government of this

with a view of protecting those who may be
attracted to this rich and fertile region, in

ment, both Canada and the United States
have precedents in previous treaties.
